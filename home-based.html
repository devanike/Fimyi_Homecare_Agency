Home-based Medical Care services you can trust

Our medical services are based on a continuum of holistic health care that promotes, maintains and restores health. In our home medical services, we focus on treatment, prevention and education. We not only serve the seniors directly but we also serve their families. We are proud to have a team of highly trained doctors across Nigeria who are happy to treat our elderly clients in the comfort of their homes.

No need to suffer any longer through the night waiting for your hospital doctor to be available for you. We can arrange to have a highly qualified doctor to come attend to you in the comfort and convenience of your home. Usually, our doctors pay visits twice in a month to ensure that all aspects of the care of your loved ones are top notch.

Our medical services include and not limited to:

  • Comprehensive care assessment.
  • Management of chronic medical conditions.
  • Referrals to specialists or service providers in other disciplines where necessary.
  • Arrangements for safe transfer for hospitalization, where necessary.
  • Prescriptions of appropriate acute and chronic medicines.
  • Arrangement for mobile diagnostics services in the comfort of your home.
  • Education of client and caregiver on the clientâ€™s medical conditions and care plan.
  • Minor medical/nursing procedures.
  • Ordering and timely interpretation of appropriate investigation.

Our medical services shall be led by a registered doctor with a valid practicing license. Your loved one will be assessed and reviewed by appropriate professional staff in accordance with professional care standards.
